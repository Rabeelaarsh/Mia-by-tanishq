Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I find my ring size?

You can find out your ring size at home with the help of the string method. Simply wrap the string around the base of your ring finger and make a mark where the two ends meet. Proceed to unwrap the string and measure the distance between the starting and ending points with a ruler. There you have your ring size!
